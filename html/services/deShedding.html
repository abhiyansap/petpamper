Professional De-shedding Treatment

Is your pet shedding excessively, leaving a trail of fur around your home? Our de-shedding treatment at Your Pet Grooming is the solution you've been looking for. We specialize in reducing shedding and promoting a healthier coat for your furry companion.

Our skilled groomers use industry-leading techniques and high-quality products to minimize shedding and improve the overall condition of your pet's coat. Say goodbye to excessive fur on your furniture and clothes with our effective de-shedding treatment.

Discover the benefits of our de-shedding service, designed to make your pet more comfortable and your home cleaner. We tailor our approach based on your pet's breed and coat type to achieve the best results.

Step 1: Coat Assessment

Our grooming experts begin by assessing your pet's coat to understand the level of shedding and identify problem areas.

Step 2: Brushing

We use specialized de-shedding tools to carefully brush and remove loose fur from your pet's coat. This helps prevent matting and tangling.

Step 3: Bathing

A thorough bath with high-quality de-shedding shampoo helps to loosen the undercoat and remove additional loose fur, promoting a healthy coat.

Step 4: Conditioning Treatment

We apply a nourishing conditioner to your pet's coat to moisturize and strengthen the fur, reducing future shedding and promoting a shiny, healthy coat.

Step 5: Blow Drying

Your pet is carefully blow-dried to remove any remaining loose fur. This step also helps to fluff the coat and leave it looking clean and vibrant.

Step 6: Final Brushing and Inspection

We perform a final brush to ensure all loose fur has been removed. Our groomers inspect the coat for any irregularities and make sure your pet looks and feels great.

Step 7: De-shedding Recommendation

Our grooming experts provide personalized recommendations to help you manage shedding between grooming sessions, including tips on at-home brushing and maintenance.
