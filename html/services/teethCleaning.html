Teeth Cleaning Service

At Your PetPamper, we prioritize the oral health of your beloved pets. Our professional teeth cleaning services are designed to ensure that your pet's teeth and gums remain in optimal condition, contributing to their overall well-being.

Step 1: Oral Examination

Our trained groomers start by conducting a thorough examination of your pet's oral health. This includes checking for signs of gum disease, tartar buildup, and any potential dental issues.

Step 2: Teeth Cleaning

We use pet-safe toothbrushes and toothpaste to gently clean your pet's teeth. Our grooming experts pay special attention to hard-to-reach areas to ensure a comprehensive cleaning process.

Step 3: Dental Rinse

After brushing, we use a dental rinse specially formulated for pets to provide additional cleaning and promote oral hygiene. This rinse helps eliminate bacteria and freshens your pet's breath.

Step 4: Inspection and Recommendations

Once the cleaning process is complete, our groomers inspect your pet's teeth and gums. If we notice any potential issues, we'll provide recommendations for further dental care and maintenance.

Step 5: Dental Care Education

We take the time to educate pet owners on the importance of regular dental care and offer tips on maintaining good oral hygiene for their pets at home.
