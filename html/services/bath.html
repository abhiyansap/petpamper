Bathing & Brushing Service

Keep your pets clean and healthy with our professional bathing and brushing services. Our experienced groomers use high-quality products to ensure your pets have a shiny coat and feel refreshed. Regular bathing and brushing help prevent matting, reduce shedding, and contribute to your pet's overall well-being.

Steps for Bathing

  1. Wet the pet thoroughly.
  2. Apply pet-friendly shampoo.
  3. Gently massage and lather.
  4. Rinse and dry.

Note: Cats may require a gentler approach during bathing.

Steps for Brushing

  1. Brush out tangles and mats.
  2. Use appropriate brushes for different coat types.
  3. Check for fleas or ticks during brushing.

Long-haired dogs may require more extensive brushing.
